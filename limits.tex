\begin{exercise}
Given that $r(v)=-2 v^2-4 v-4$, evaluate $r(-0.4)$. Express your answer in decimal notation.
\begin{hint}
$r(-0.4)=-2 (-0.4)^2-4 (-0.4)-4$.
\end{hint}
\begin{hint}
$r(-0.4)=-2.72$.
\end{hint}
The value of the function $r(v)=-2 v^2-4 v-4$, evaluated at $v=-0.4$, is $\answer{-2.72}$.
\end{exercise}
